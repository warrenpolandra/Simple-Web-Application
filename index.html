Hawthorne Island

A remote and luxurious island, only for the finest dining

A barrier island near Savannah. Nordic architecture-style island: organic modernism, where the shapes are inspired by nature—rock walls or sweeping glass bay windows. This island also has some path with white limestone. Every single building in this island is covered with concrete, starting from the Dining Hall, The Warehouse, even the Chefs' houses. You can clearly see the façade of the restaurant as well as enough hallway. This island is specifically designed for the finest dining in the world.

To get to this island, you'll have to get on a boat and travels 5 miles from Savannah. Upon reaching this island, you'll be greeted by our reception and will immediately start your exclusively prepared island tour. You'll see all of the spectacular views in this island and all of the buildings here. This island consist of several buildings, such as the Main Dining Hall where you'll eat your 13 course dishes, the warehouse where all of the ingredients are stored, and the Head Chef House.

Chefs

The people who creates the masterpiece

Julian Slowik

Head Chef

Starting his culinary journey in a diner as a cook, Julian would go on to become a famous chef with his own private island. He was awarded a 3 star as michelin chef. Now he is the current and always will be the head chef of Hawthorne Kitchen.

Jeremy Louden

Sous Chef

Jeremy found his culinary passion in Paris, where he honed his skills under the guidance of a legendary chef known for his creations. Jeremy's signature dish, the "Louden Surprise," has become a favorite among the patrons.

Katherine Keller

Sous Chef

Rumor has it that Katherine spent years traveling the world, gathering all sorts of culinary secrets from remote villages and hidden markets, infusing her uncountable dishes with a global tapestry of flavors that leave diners spellbound.

Gourmand Tyler

Commis Chef

A loyal customer of Hawthorne Kitchen, obsessed with Julian Slowik's culinary. Upon Slowik's sudden departure, Tyler embarked on a daring journey to emulate the famed chef's dishes, determined to honor the flavors that he amazed for so long.

Menu

Over the next few hour since you stepped your foot into the Dining Hall, You will ingest fat, salt, sugar, protein, bacteria, fungi, various plants and animals and even an entire ecosystem. This 13-course meal will satisfy your hunger, designed by yours truly, Chef Julian Slowik

Lemon Caviar Oyster

An oyster delight topped with zesty lemon caviar, offering a perfect blend of briny and citrus sensations in one exquisite bite. It's a culinary masterpiece that invigorates the palate.

Amuse Bouche

The Amuse Bouche is a bite-sized culinary creation designed to tantalize the taste buds and ignite the dining experience. It serves as a flavorful and artistic appetizer, setting the tone for the culinary journey to come.

The Island

"The Island" is a gastronomic adventure that transports diners exotic flavors. This masterpiece features a blend of tropical ingredients, offering a taste of paradise with each bite.

Breadless Bread
